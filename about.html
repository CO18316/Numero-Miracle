Methods In Numerology

Basic mathematical operation are used in numerology, wherein the numbers are reduced by simple addition. For example, number 15, for instance, is reduced to 6 to adding 1 & 5 (i.e. 1 + 5 = 6) Similarly, 1974 is reduced to 3 ( 1 + 9 + 7 + 4 = 21 and then 21 is 2 + 1 = 3 ) Digit Summing Practitioners of Numerology / Numerologists generally made a numerology chart that involves reducing a number or a word by a process known as Digit Summing and reach on conclusions based on the single digit derived. Digit Summing involves taking the sum of all of the digits in the number and repeating the method as needed until a single-digit answer is produced. Several methods of calculation are practiced including Chaldean, Pythagorean, Hebraic, Helyn Hitchcock's, Phonetic, Japanese and Indian. In some cases, the name and birth date of an individual are used to analyze and define personality and propensities
